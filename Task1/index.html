It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Founded in 1971, Starbucks is a coffee company and coffeehouse chain based in Seattle, Washington. With more than 24,000 locations worldwide, it is one of the largest coffee chains in the world. The company sells coffee, tea, sandwiches, and other food items, as well as coffee mugs and other merchandise. Starbucks is known for its signature coffee drinks, such as the Frappuccino and the Pike Place Roast. The company also offers a variety of non-coffee beverages, such as hot chocolate and iced tea. In addition to its coffeehouses, Starbucks has a grocery store division, a coffee roasting operation, and a line of bottled coffee drinks.
